MIẾN LƯƠNG

Ingredients
Eel (cleaned)

Vermicelli noodles (miến)

Kaffir lime leaves and ginger leaves (for steaming option)

Turmeric, shallots, betel leaves (for marinated and grilled option)

Garlic, chili, fish sauce, sugar, salt, pepper

Herbs and fresh greens for garnish

Cooking Options for Eel
Traditional method: Fry eel until crispy.

Alternative 1: Steam eel with kaffir lime leaves and ginger leaves to add fragrance and reduce fishy smell.

Alternative 2: Marinate eel with turmeric and shallots, then grill wrapped in betel leaves for an aromatic twist.

Instructions
Prepare eel by cleaning thoroughly.

Choose one cooking method: fry, steam, or marinate and grill.

Prepare vermicelli noodles as usual.

Cook broth or sauce to serve with vermicelli and eel.

Assemble the dish by combining vermicelli noodles, cooked eel, herbs, and broth or sauce.

Notes
These variations enhance the fragrance and flavor of the eel and keep it fresh.

The dish is nutritious and great for warming up during colder days.
