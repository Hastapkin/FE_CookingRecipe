NEM THÍNH

Ingredients
Pork (boiled and thinly sliced)

Pork skin (chewy, thinly sliced)

Toasted rice powder (thính gạo rang) — golden and fragrant, key to the dish's flavor

Fresh herbs: fig leaves, lime leaves, Thai basil, spearmint

Dipping sauce made with fish sauce, garlic, and chili

Preparation
Boil pork until tender, then slice thinly.

Thinly slice pork skin to add chewy texture.

Coat pork slices generously with toasted rice powder, which provides a nutty aroma and crispiness.

Serve with fresh herbs that refresh and balance the rich flavors.

Accompany with a flavorful dipping sauce blending fish sauce, garlic, and chili to enhance taste.

Cultural Context
Nem Thính﻿ is cherished as a traditional Vietnamese snack, combining unique textures and harmonious flavors.

It showcases rustic culinary artistry through its signature use of toasted rice powder and fresh herbs.
